About us

Gaurav More

Gaurav More, Founder of Studio TC, is not your run-of-the-mill wedding photographer. All his pictures are stylish, full of romance, and show his enjoyment of life and people. He is someone who specializes in making your special moments memorable. His pictures will occupy pride of place in your homes and hearts. Not because he is good, he is, but because he sees you the way the world sees you. Gaurav works on his pictures to get more variations and frames.

His sense of class and style makes him one of the best wedding photographers in India today. This fun, open, witty, unassuming, friendly photographer in Maharashtra loves to forge relationships with his clients.

  • He is a designer by profession
  • a photographer by aptitude

  • Gaurav uses his imagination, creativity, experience,and professionalism to capture your important moments
